Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order? +

Browse products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account first.

What are the delivery charges? +

Delivery is Rs. 200 for orders under Rs. 2000. Free delivery for orders above Rs. 2000.

How fresh are the products? +

All products are harvested within 24 hours of delivery, ensuring maximum freshness.

Can I become a seller? +

Yes! Visit our Seller Onboarding page to register as a farmer and start selling your produce.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept cash on delivery, credit/debit cards, and mobile payments.
