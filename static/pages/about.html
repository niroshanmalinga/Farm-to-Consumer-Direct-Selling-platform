About FarmDirect

Connecting Sri Lankan farmers directly with consumers for a fresher, fairer future

Our Mission

FarmDirect was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to eliminate middlemen and create direct connections between local farmers and consumers across Sri Lanka.

We believe that everyone deserves access to fresh, organic produce at fair prices, while farmers deserve fair compensation for their hard work.

By leveraging technology, we're building a sustainable ecosystem that benefits both producers and consumers.

Our Values

Quality First

We ensure every product meets our strict organic and freshness standards.

Fair Trade

Fair prices for farmers and affordable rates for customers.

Sustainability

Promoting eco-friendly farming practices for a greener future.
